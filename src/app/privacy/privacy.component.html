Privacy Policy

The Computer Reset liquidation is being run by volunteers to assist the family of Richard Byron, former owner of Computer Reset in Dallas, TX.

Information we retain:
  • If you log into this site, we retain your Facebook first name, last name and unique ID.
  • If you sign up for an event, we retain the information used to sign up for the event. This includes your real name, city and state and any attendance information.
  • We do not sell any data. All data is retained only for use of liquidation events and to comply with current and future Covid-19 governmental requirements.
  • We do not share any data with any marketing partners.
