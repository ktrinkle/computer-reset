No climbing or using the ladders - We will have volunteers available to help you get stuff down from 2nd and 3rd level pallet shelves. 5. Clean up after yourself. If you open and search a box, put everything back in it and put it back where it was before moving on. If you're drinking something, toss the bottle/cup in the trash, not the bushes. 6. Stay out of taped off and off-limits areas. 7. No photos, livestreaming, or filming without permission. 8. You are responsible for your own safety. If a volunteer tells you to take a time out in front of a fan or to get some water, you will do it. 10. Don't make us make more rules. Cash and PayPal accepted. Parking will be in front for guests, in back for volunteers. When you pay you'll be able to pull around back and load up your pile.
